Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

1. Introduction

VideoUploader ("the App") is a personal automation tool for uploading videos to social media platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Access

VideoUploader accesses the following information from your TikTok account:

  • Permission to post videos on your behalf
  • Basic account information required for authentication

3. How We Use Your Information

The information accessed is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Post videos to your TikTok profile on your behalf

4. Data Storage

VideoUploader does not store any personal data, TikTok credentials, or account information on external servers. All authentication tokens are stored locally on your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any of your personal information or TikTok account data to third parties.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You can revoke VideoUploader's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings.
